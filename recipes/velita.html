Vela Herb-Crusted Salmon

An elegant and flavorful dish featuring salmon fillets coated in a fragrant mixture of fresh herbs, breadcrumbs, and citrus zest. The crust forms a golden, crispy layer that complements the tender, flaky salmon underneath. This dish is typically baked to perfection, locking in moisture and creating a harmonious blend of textures and tastes

The herb crust provides a vibrant, aromatic profile with hints of lemon, parsley, and thyme, enhancing the natural richness of the salmon. Often served with a side of seasonal vegetables or a light salad, this dish is ideal for a sophisticated dinner yet simple enough for a weeknight meal.

Ingredients

  • Salmon Fillets
  • Breadcrumbs
  • Fresh Herbs (like parsley, thyme, and rosemary)
  • Lemon Zest
  • Olive Oil
  • Salt and Pepper
