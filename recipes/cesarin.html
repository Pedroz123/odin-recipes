Ceasar Salad

The Caesar salad is a classic, crisp salad known for its bold flavors and rich dressing. Traditionally, it features romaine lettuce, crunchy croutons, and a savory dressing made from garlic, anchovies, lemon juice, Dijon mustard, and Parmesan cheese, often emulsified with egg yolk or mayonnaise.

Itâ€™s believed to have originated in the 1920s, created by Italian-American restaurateur Caesar Cardini in Tijuana, Mexico. Today, the Caesar salad is a popular choice for its balance of tangy, salty, and umami flavors.

Ingredients

  • Romaine Lettuce
  • Croutons
  • Garlic
  • Anchovy Fillets or Paste
  • Lemon Juice
  • Parmesan Cheese

Steps

  1. Toss the romaine lettuce with the Caesar dressing until well-coated
  2. Add the croutons and toss lightly
  3. Garnish with extra Parmesan cheese and optional toppings if desired
